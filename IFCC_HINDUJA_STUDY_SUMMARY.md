## 🎯 Key Value of This Study

### 1. **Third Independent Validation**
- Validates liver enzymes (ALT, AST, GGT) from Apollo and North Indian studies
- Provides additional confidence in Indian-specific ranges

### 2. **New Parameters Not in Previous Studies**
- **Immunoglobulins:** IgG, IgA, IgM
- **Complement:** C3, C4
- **Electrolytes:** Sodium, Potassium, Chloride, Calcium
- **Other:** Iron (Fe), Transthyretin (TTR), Creatine Kinase (CK)

### 3. **Identifies Bias-Prone Parameters**
10 analytes showed significant bias and required recalibration:
- Glucose
- Total Cholesterol
- LDL-C
- Creatinine
- AST
- ALT
- GGT
- Total Bilirubin
- C3
- C4

**Clinical Impact:** These parameters need special validation against real-world Indian EHR data.

### 4. **Confirms Age/Gender Partitioning Requirements**
- **Gender partitioning required:** TC, HDL-C, TG, hs-CRP, Albumin, AST, ALT, ALP, GGT, Total Bilirubin, Urea, Creatinine, Uric Acid, Iron, Transthyretin, CK, IgM
- **Age partitioning required:** Glucose, Albumin, Total Cholesterol, ALP, Urea
